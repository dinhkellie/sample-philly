Sample Philly is an application based on Kara Crombie’s interactive sculpture in Philadelphia, PA as a part of the city-wide Monument Lab project. It aims to honor Philadelphia’s rich musical history by allowing users to produce their own musical compositions from a vast archive of songs recorded in and about the great city of Philadelphia.
